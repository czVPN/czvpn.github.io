About Us

czVPN is a newly launched VPN service, launched by a small group of developers who meet regularaly in an IRC channel. We are long users of VPN services and we have over the years notice the lack of speed and security of most common VPN services, together with this evidence we are working to provide a service with everything that other VPN providers do not provide. Our main goal is to provide stronger methods of encryption, faster speeds, along with an easy to use interface. Along with all of thise, we will have very strict measures on how we deal with logging, which we believe to be one of the most important feautrs of any service.

One of the most notable things at make us different from other VPN services is the option to purchase a private VPN server that only you are able to connect to, these private servers enable greater speeds for those looking to hide their IP without lowering their download speed at an substantial amount. The common VPN service will only offer servers shared by all clients, this causes the server to throttle download speeds, and overall decreases the performance.

For a more detailed report on how we handle logging, please refer to our privacy policies.
